// How the Pipeline Works

From raw video to viral-ready clips in 6 steps

01

Upload & Extract Audio

Your video is uploaded and FFmpeg extracts a high-quality audio track (16kHz mono WAV) for transcription.

FFmpeg
02

Transcribe with AI

OpenAI Whisper converts speech to text with precise word-level timestamps. Choose from tiny (fastest) to large (most accurate).

OpenAI Whisper
03

Generate Embeddings

Each transcript segment is converted into a 384-dimensional semantic vector, capturing the meaning of the content.

Sentence Transformers
04

Detect Topics & Boundaries

Using cosine similarity between embeddings, the system identifies where topics shift. This prevents clips from awkwardly cutting mid-topic.

Cosine Similarity
05

Score & Rank Clips

Candidate clips are scored based on text quality, internal coherence (how well it flows), and distinctiveness (unique content). LLM mode adds AI-powered ranking.

Multi-factor Scoring
06

Render & Download

Top clips are extracted from the original video using FFmpeg with optimized encoding (H.264 + AAC). Download and share!

FFmpeg

Scoring Modes Explained

Coherence

Prioritizes internal flow. Best for interviews, conversations, podcasts.

60% coherence | 20% text | 20% unique
Topic

Stays within topic boundaries. Best for lectures, tutorials, presentations.

40% unique | 30% coherence | 30% text
Combined

Balanced approach with topic-crossing penalty. Best for general content.

40% coherence | 30% unique | 30% text
LLM

AI analyzes transcript and picks the most engaging moments. Best quality.

40% AI rank | 20% each other

LLM mode sends transcript to the selected provider for intelligent topic segmentation and ranking.

[SECURE] Your API key is never logged or stored. It is only used in-memory for this request and sent directly to the provider.
